Mads Baungaard

Hello! I'm a visiting postdoc at the University of Oxford, where I study wind farm flows.
Before that, I used to work at DTU Wind and Energy Systems.

Research interests

  • Computational fluid dynamics (CFD)
  • Turbulence modeling for RANS
    • Two-equation models
    • Explicit algebraic Reynolds stress modeling (EARSM)
  • Canonical flows (homogeneos shear flow, channel, square duct, cavity, etc.)
  • Large-eddy simulations
  • Wind turbine aerodynamics
  • Actuator disk (AD) and actuator line (AL) modelling of wind turbines
